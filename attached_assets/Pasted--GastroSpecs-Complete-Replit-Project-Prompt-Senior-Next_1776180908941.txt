## WHAT GASTROSPECS IS вҖ” READ THIS COMPLETELY

GastroSpecs is a **global specialist procurement agency** for commercial kitchen and butchery equipment. Based in Palestine, serving clients worldwide.

**What they do:**
- Source commercial kitchen equipment (refrigerators, freezers, ice cream machines, grills, meat slicers, ovens, dishwashers, cold rooms) directly from certified Chinese factories
- Provide professional kitchen layout design using CAD software
- Deliver worldwide via **DDP вҖ” Delivered Duty Paid** (they handle factory order, shipping, customs clearance, import duties, and last-mile delivery вҖ” client just receives the equipment at their door)

**Three services:**
1. **Single Product Sourcing** вҖ” one machine, any quantity from 1 unit, best factory price, DDP delivered worldwide
2. **Full Kitchen Consultancy** вҖ” layout design + complete equipment package for new restaurant/hotel openings
3. **Bulk Orders** вҖ” same spec across many units, volume pricing, consolidated DDP shipping for chains and franchises

**Key competitive advantages:**
- Factory-direct pricing (20вҖ“40% cheaper than Alibaba or local suppliers)
- Technical expertise (knows motor specs, steel grades, CE/SASO certifications)
- DDP worldwide shipping вҖ” removes all import complexity for the buyer
- Local after-sales technicians in target markets
- Transparent 10% commission вҖ” client sees the real factory price

**Target clients:** Restaurants, hotels, butcheries, supermarkets, food chains, ghost kitchens вҖ” in Saudi Arabia, UAE, Qatar, Jordan, Egypt, Kenya, Europe, and worldwide
